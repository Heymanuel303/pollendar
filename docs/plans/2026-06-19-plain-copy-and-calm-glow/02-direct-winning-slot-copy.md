## Context
The plan "plain-copy-and-calm-glow" makes Pollendar feel calmer and less AI-written: it dials the glow/bloom visual effect down to near-flat (other phases) and rewrites UI + email copy into a plain, direct voice. This phase owns the most visible piece of metaphor copy: the "✦ In bloom" label that marks the winning slot, plus the two bloom-metaphor microcopy lines tied to results (the empty hint and the score caption). The ✦ sparkle stays; only the words change ("In bloom" → "Top pick"). The CSS classes (`.bloom`, `.bloom-bg`), component names (`BestSlotBloom`, `BestSlotBadge`), and `data-testid`s stay exactly as they are; only the words a user reads change.

## Objective
